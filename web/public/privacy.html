TuneTrix Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11 October 2025

TuneTrix (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains the limited way we handle information for the TuneTrix website, desktop app, and mobile app.

What we collect

We do not collect account details, analytics, tracking information, or persistent identifiers. The only user data we receive is the audio content that you explicitly upload while using the TuneTrix tools.

How uploads are used

  • Uploaded stems are processed temporarily to let you audition gating, pitch, and mixing behaviour.
  • Files are stored only long enough for the session in which you upload them and are not shared with third parties.
  • You remain the owner of all content you upload.

Data retention

Uploads are automatically discarded once your session ends or after processing completes. We do not archive, back up, or otherwise retain your files beyond that window.

Security

We use modern transport security (HTTPS) and limit access to the processing pipeline to protect uploaded media. No additional personally identifying information is captured.

Children’s privacy

TuneTrix is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided uploads, please contact us so we can delete the material promptly.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@tunetrix.app.

Updates

We may revise this policy to reflect new functionality. Material changes will be posted at this location, and the “Effective date” above will identify the latest version.
